About NebriX

AI-Driven Satellite Health Monitoring & Anomaly Detection

🌌 Project Overview

NebriX is developing an AI/ML-based platform to proactively monitor satellite telemetry, detect anomalies in real-time, and provide early alerts. By reducing operational risks, extending satellite lifespan, and offering scalability across multiple missions, NebriX aligns with ISRO’s vision of reliable space technology.

🎯 Objective

Develop a proactive monitoring and anomaly detection platform using AI/ML.

🚀 Key Benefits

Early fault detection, longer satellite life, reduced risks, and scalability.

⭐ USP

A unique AI-enabled solution with a built-in training simulator for students.

🌠 Our Vision

At NebriX, we believe the future of space exploration depends on the synergy of artificial intelligence and human ingenuity. Our mission is to empower satellite operations with intelligent, reliable, and accessible solutions — bridging innovation with education for the next generation of space scientists.
