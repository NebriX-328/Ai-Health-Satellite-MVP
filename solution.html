🚨 The Problem

Satellites are critical assets for communication, navigation, weather forecasting, and defense. However, they face multiple challenges that threaten their health and mission success:

  • ⚡ Telemetry anomalies that go unnoticed until failure occurs
  • 🛰️ Unexpected hardware malfunctions reducing satellite lifespan
  • 🌌 Space environment hazards such as radiation & temperature extremes
  • 📉 Lack of proactive monitoring → delayed fault detection and high operational risks

💡 The NebriX Solution

To address these challenges, NebriX introduces an AI-driven Satellite Health Monitoring & Anomaly Detection System. Our platform leverages cutting-edge AI/ML algorithms to:

  • 🔍 Continuously monitor satellite telemetry in real time
  • 📊 Detect anomalies proactively before critical failure occurs
  • ⚠️ Generate instant alerts to ground stations for corrective actions
  • 🛠️ Extend satellite lifespan with predictive maintenance
  • 🌍 Provide a training simulator for students & researchers to understand satellite health management

✨ USP: A proactive, AI-enabled approach aligning with ISRO’s needs while empowering future engineers.
